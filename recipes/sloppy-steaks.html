Sloppy Steaks

Sloppy Steaks offer a unique and unexpected take on the classic grilled steak experience. Instead of traditional steak sauces or marinades, this inventive dish features succulent grilled steaks topped with a refreshing glass of water poured directly onto them. It's a culinary adventure that combines the savory richness of perfectly grilled meat with the crisp, clean taste of water, resulting in a surprisingly satisfying and hydrating dining experience.

ingredients include:

  • Grilled steak
  • Glass of water
  • Balls of steel
